About Us

Get to know about us!

This is WHO WE ARE!

Gururkul, the name itself says to dream big and work for achieving it. With more than one and a half decades of experience in the field of coaching and guidance, the class is a popular name among science students. Gururkul is the first academy that came up with a new way of learning, EXPERIMENTAL LEARNING.

We believe,” We learn by doing because we do by learning.”With the growing and intense competition that the students face these days, just not about scoring and passing the exams that matter; and that is where we provide the students the edge to overcome the hurdles that they will be facing in the careers that they choose.

What makes us EXCEPTIONAL!

We have our own labs, (SCIENCE LAB, CHEMISTRY LAB) During their weekly science lab sessions, students conduct a variety of experiments designed to incorporate kinesthetic and experiential learning of subjects covered in class. Science Lab sessions help bring the curriculum to life! This is an essential part of fulfilling Gururkul’s mission to develop scientifically-curious students.

The Chemistry lab of Gururkul has a wide range of chemicals available with them to perform different experiments. Chemistry lab is the required component of all chemistry courses not only to give experiments their due place in the subject but also to promote in general, an experimental culture in the school system. In the chemistry lab, students are expected to indulge in a creative interplay of observation, experimentation, and theoretical inferences. Students are trained to use scientific instruments, make observations, interpret and draw conclusions from observed facts.
